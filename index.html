Welcome to the
Ideathon 2021!

Ideathon is a competitive event where participating teams are required to develop a solution using Google technologies for one or more of the 17 United Nations Sustainable Development Goals.

Each team is to be made up of 2 to 4 students.

The event welcomes all PSB Academy students and members from every Singapore DSCs.

Winners of the event stand to win up to SGD$50 Hachi.tech e-Gift card with some cool Google Developer swags!

Note: The event will be conducted in SGT time (GMT +8). Also, there will be a registration fee for each team ($10).
